Introduction

POP3 and IMAP of the TCP/IP family are profoundly creative ideas.
Their birth makes our life and work become more closely connected.

The convenience, depends solely on your choice, naturally there will be. If you often hesitate to select a large number of email attachments, such as school homework, workplace resumes, unit assignment materials and other life situations, do you expect to have a convenient and efficient tool on PC which assists you to download batch attachments?

In the traditional way, we may have to log in mailbox client, fill in some information, and then start the download journey. Now we can bypass these processes and enjoy a more convenient experience with simple configuration.

Email Attachments Download is portable, efficient, secure, and most importantly, completely open source and free.
