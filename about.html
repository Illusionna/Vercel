About

Product Orientation

Our products are suitable for any group, anyone can easily use it, especially in schools, workplaces, units and other need to collect email batch attachments, the advantages of our products will be profoundly played.

Product Name

The kernel name is Senu, an eagle, which comes from Assassin's Creed Origins hero Bayek's helper. We wanna assist you to work efficiently with a powerful kernel. The product is called NEUmail originating in our undergraduate Northeastern University. We hope that the Email Attachment Download tool will serve the society and help more people.

Potential Risk

The Windows operating system misidentifies NEUmail as a Trojan virus. The relay transmission process of POP3 and IMAP protocols is illegally stolen by hackers.
